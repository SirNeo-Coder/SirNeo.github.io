Future Direction

Cielito Zamora Senior High School (CZSHS) Robotics Organization is a group whose members focuses on engaging and exciting mentor-based programs which build science, engineering, mathematics, and technology skills, that inspire innovation and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence. It is aligned with the objective of the robotics field to create intelligent machines that can assist humans in a variety of ways.

In the year 2020 in the midst of the global pandemic, it started as a small club out of interest and passion as a result of the Division Training of Robotics Trainers using the EV3 Core Set at SDO Caloocan last March 2020.

Since then, it teaches robotics in a fun and easy manner to senior high school students to participate in related conventions and national competitions in order to create awareness of technology.

It addresses practical use and benefits of robotics in education to include more real life applications, such as social interaction and skills; improve critical thinking skills and problem solving capabilities; improve reasoning and skills related to physics and math; higher levels of engagement and improvement in teamwork and interpersonal skills.
