Vision and Mission

Vision

All students design, innovate, and embrace STEM and ICT-Programming to solve current community problems.

Mission

To increase student interest and involvement in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) by engaging students in hands-on, affordable, and sustainable robotics engineering program.

To provide a safe, happy, and secure learning environment that empowers students to reach their full potential while nurturing their self-confidence and leadership skills.
